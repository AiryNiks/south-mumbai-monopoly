## Strategic Property Investment Tips

### Best Value Properties
1. **Orange Properties** - Most frequently landed on, moderate cost
2. **Red Properties** - High rent potential
3. **Railway Stations** - Consistent income with minimal investment

### High-Risk/High-Reward
1. **Dark Blue Properties** - Highest rents but expensive
2. **Green Properties** - Good balance of cost and rent

### Defensive Purchases
1. **Pink/Light Blue** - Block opponents from monopolies
2. **Yellow** - Strategic blockade in mid-game

### Early Game Strategy
1. Buy brown properties to start cash flow
2. Acquire railways when possible
3. Complete one color group for advantage

### Late Game Strategy
1. Build hotels on highest-rent properties
2. Mortgage low-income properties for cash
3. Focus on blocking opponents from creating monopolies
